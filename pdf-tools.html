Frequently Asked Questions

Are all these PDF tools really free?

Yes — 100% free, no sign-up, no watermark, no file size limits imposed by servers. Processing happens in your browser using PDF-lib and PDF.js.

Do my PDF files get uploaded to a server?

Never. All PDF operations — merge, compress, split, edit — happen locally in your browser. Your documents stay on your device.

Which PDF tool should I use to reduce file size?

Use Compress PDF to reduce image-heavy PDFs. Use Delete Pages to remove unnecessary pages first, then compress for best results.

Can I use these tools on iPhone and Android?

Yes — all PDF tools are optimized for mobile. Tap the tool card to open it on your phone or tablet.

Are there any page limits?

No hard limits. Practical limits depend on your device RAM. Most modern phones handle PDFs up to 100 pages easily.
