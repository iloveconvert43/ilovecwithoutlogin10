About Crop PDF

Remove excessive margins from scanned documents, presentations, and academic papers. Set a custom crop box to trim whitespace from all pages at once. Ideal for making PDFs more readable on tablets and e-readers.

Crop boxes are applied in-browser — no server processing, no upload, complete privacy.

How to Use — Step by Step

1

Upload PDF

Upload the PDF with margins to trim.

2

Set Crop Box

Enter margin values (top, right, bottom, left) in millimeters or pixels.

3

Preview

See the crop area applied to a sample page.

4

Crop & Download

Click Crop PDF to apply to all pages and download.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I crop individual pages differently?

Yes — switch to per-page mode to set different crop boxes for each page.

Does cropping permanently remove content?

Cropping sets a "crop box" in the PDF — the hidden content technically still exists in the file.

Will the PDF look different in different viewers?

Most PDF viewers respect the crop box and hide cropped areas.

Can I undo a crop?

Yes — some PDF viewers can reveal the full page. Or re-upload your original.

Is this useful for scanned books?

Absolutely — book scans often have large gray borders that waste screen space.
