🎯 Our Mission

iLoveConvert was created with one goal: make powerful file conversion and editing tools freely accessible to everyone. Whether you need to convert a PDF, compress an image, generate a QR code, or format JSON — we've got you covered, right in your browser, with zero friction.

🔒 Your Privacy Matters

Every single tool on iLoveConvert processes your files locally in your browser. Your documents, images, and videos never leave your device — they are never uploaded to any server. We don't collect, store, or sell any user data. Your files are 100% private.

🆓 Free Forever

All tools are free to use with no account required and no usage limits. We're supported by non-intrusive advertising. We will never put your essential tools behind a paywall.

⚙️ Built With Modern Technology

We use cutting-edge browser APIs — Web Crypto API, Canvas API, MediaRecorder, Web Workers, PDF.js, Fabric.js, PDF-lib, and more. Everything runs client-side for maximum speed, privacy, and reliability. No server, no waiting, no limits.
